# Customer Go-Live Announcement

You are creating a customer go-live announcement when a customer deploys your product to production.

## Your Task

Create celebratory, metrics-driven social media posts (and optional blog) that highlight customer achievements when they launch in production.

## What is a Go-Live?

**Customer Go-Live** = Announcement when a customer launches your product in production

This is NOT a pilot or beta. This is real production deployment with real users and real business impact.

## Deliverables

- **Social media post** (LinkedIn, 4-7 sentences, 100-150 words) - PRIMARY
- **Visual asset** (optional, quote card with key metric)
- **Blog post** (optional, 400-600 words for major go-lives)

## Process

1. **Gather Information**
   Ask the user:
   - [ ] Customer name
   - [ ] Product(s) deployed
   - [ ] Key metrics or results (before/after, volume, scale)
   - [ ] Why this is significant (regulated industry, scale, innovation)
   - [ ] Stakeholders to tag/mention
   - [ ] Any customer quotes available?
   - [ ] Timeline: When did they go live?

2. **Use These Resources**
   - **Agent Instructions**: `/agents/customer-go-live-writer.md`
   - **Template**: `/templates/customer-go-live-template.md`
   - **Brand Guidelines**: `/brand/voice-and-tone.md`, `/brand/terminology.md`
   - **Examples**: Check `/examples/customer-go-lives/` for patterns

3. **Social Post Structure** (4-7 sentences)

   **Hook (1-2 sentences):** Congratulations + what they launched. Be specific.

   **Context (2-3 sentences):** Why this matters. What problem it solves. What makes it significant.

   **Impact (1-2 sentences):** Key metrics or results. Demonstrate trust, scale, or value.

   **Close (1 sentence):** Forward-looking statement. Call out stakeholders.

4. **Writing Principles**

   Do: Lead with customer's achievement, make them the hero, include specific numbers, use active voice, be celebratory.

   Don't: Generic congratulations, lead with product features, marketing jargon, make it about your company instead of the customer.

5. **Metrics Matter**
   - Before/after: "10% → 100% coverage"
   - Volume: "97,000 interviews completed"
   - Percentage improvements: "40% faster"
   - Time savings: "Reduced from 3 days to 30 minutes"
   - Scale: "Serving 50,000+ employees"

## Timeline

**Typical**: 24-48 hours from go-live to publication. This is time-sensitive!

## Examples of Strong Hooks

Good: "Congrats to Acme Corp on launching AI Service Agent to 97,000 HR interviews, moving from 10% to 100% coverage in just weeks."

Weak: "We're excited to announce that another customer has gone live with our product."

- Tag relevant stakeholders for visibility
- Post during business hours (9am-5pm optimal)
- Monitor engagement and respond to comments
- Share internally for sales enablement
