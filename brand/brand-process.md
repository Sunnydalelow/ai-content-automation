## Brand Review

All **net-new, high-visibility, or big-budget work** should go through brand review before publication.

---

## Tier System

Determines whether brand review is required:

| Tier | Type | Review Required? |
|------|------|-----------------|
| **Tier 1** | Global campaigns, major launches, new brand elements | Yes |
| **Tier 2** | Product launches, regional campaigns, major content | Yes |
| **Tier 3** | Extensions of existing approved campaigns | No (but encouraged) |
| **Tier 4** | Templated items (emails, standard blog posts) | No |

---

## Review Process

### Step 1: Submit
- Project description and timeline
- Creative assets (drafts, mockups)
- Key stakeholders

### Step 2: Review
- Brand team reviews against guidelines
- Initial feedback provided (target: 1 business day)

### Step 3: Collaborate
- Iterative rounds as needed
- Brand team partners with you to refine

### Step 4: Launch
- Brand approved = ready to publish
- Approval documented

---

## Timeline

Allow at least **1 week** for the full review process. Build brand review into your project timeline early.
