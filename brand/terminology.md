## Use This, Not That

Clear language over jargon -- always.

| Use This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| use | leverage |
| teamwork | synergy |
| fast | accelerate |
| improve | optimize |
| help | enable/empower |
| work together | collaborate |
| change | transform (unless truly transformative) |

---

## Words to Avoid

These buzzwords and overused phrases dilute your message:

- disrupt / disruptive
- paradigm shift
- revolutionary
- game-changing
- best-in-class
- world-class
- next-generation
- cutting-edge
- synergy
- leverage (as a verb)

**Instead:** Be specific. Describe actual capabilities and measurable benefits.

## Acronyms and Abbreviations

### First Use Rule
**Always spell out on first use**, then use acronym.

**Example:**

## Technical Terms

### For Business Audiences
Use plain language:
- Instead of "API integration" say "Connect your apps"
- Instead of "Deploy to production" say "Launch to your team"

### For Technical Audiences
Use accurate technical terminology freely.

### For Mixed Audiences
Layer complexity: start accessible, add technical detail for those who want it.
