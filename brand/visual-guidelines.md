## Document Formatting

### Headlines
- **H1:** Main title only, once per document
- **H2:** Major sections
- **H3:** Subsections within H2
- **H4+:** Use sparingly

### Capitalization
- **Sentence case** for most headlines
- **Title Case** for major announcements or formal documents

### Text Formatting
- **Bold:** Emphasis, product names, key terms
- **Italic:** Rare; for emphasis or publication titles
- **Underline:** Links only
- **ALL CAPS:** Avoid in body copy

---

## Content Length Guidelines

### Blogs (Short-form)
- **Total:** 600-1,000 words
- **Paragraphs:** 3-4 sentences
- **Sentences:** 15-20 words average

### Customer Stories
- **Total:** 800-1,200 words
- **Quotes:** 2-4 customer quotes minimum

### Whitepapers (Long-form)
- **Total:** 3,000-5,000 words
- **Executive summary:** 200-300 words standalone

### SEO Pages
- **Total:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **FAQ section:** Required

### Social Media
- **LinkedIn:** 150-300 characters (can expand to 1,300)
- **Twitter/X:** 100-280 characters

## Accessibility Requirements

### Must-Haves
- [ ] Alt text for all images
- [ ] Proper heading hierarchy (no skipped levels)
- [ ] AA contrast ratio compliance
- [ ] Descriptive link text (not "click here")
- [ ] Captions for video content
- [ ] Screen reader compatibility

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Using multiple H1 tags
- Skipping heading levels (H2 to H4)
- Walls of text without breaks
- Generic link text ("click here", "learn more")
- Missing alt text on images
- Non-compliant color contrast
