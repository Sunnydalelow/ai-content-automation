## Tone Guidelines

### General Tone
- **Professional yet approachable** -- We're experts, but not elitist
- **Optimistic but realistic** -- Enthusiastic without hype
- **Confident but humble** -- Strong claims backed by proof
- **Human and conversational** -- Not robotic or overly formal

### Tone by Content Type

#### Blogs (Short-form)
- **Tone:** Conversational, energetic, accessible
- **Style:** Quick-hitting insights, scannable format

#### Customer Stories
- **Tone:** Authentic, inspiring, results-focused
- **Style:** Narrative-driven with concrete outcomes

#### Whitepapers (Long-form)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, educational, thorough
- **Style:** In-depth analysis with expert insights

#### SEO Pages
- **Tone:** Helpful, informative, optimized
- **Style:** Comprehensive coverage of topic

#### Social Media
- **Tone:** Energetic, engaging, timely
- **Style:** Punchy, visual, conversation-starting

## Writing Style

### Sentence Structure
- **Keep it short:** Aim for 15-20 words average
- **Maximum:** 25 words per sentence
- **Vary length:** Mix short punchy sentences with longer ones for rhythm
- **Active voice preferred:** "Teams use [Product]" not "[Product] is used by teams"

### Paragraph Structure
- **Keep it brief:** 3-4 sentences average
- **Maximum:** 5-6 sentences
- **One idea per paragraph:** Don't cram multiple concepts together
- **Use white space:** Break up text for scannability
