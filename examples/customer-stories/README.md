# Customer Story Examples

This directory contains example customer stories that demonstrate the proof-oriented format.

## Key Elements to Study

- Opening with universal problem (NOT company background)
- Results delivered early (paragraph 2)
- Solution broken into multiple subsections
- Validation section with methodology
- Quotes integrated into narrative flow
- Specific metrics throughout

## Adding Your Own Examples

Add your best published customer stories here. Include the analysis notes at the bottom to help the AI understand what makes each story effective.
