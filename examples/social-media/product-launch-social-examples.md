# Example Social Media: Product Launch Campaign

**Campaign:** AI Agent Platform Launch
**Platforms:** LinkedIn + Twitter/X
**Goal:** Awareness + Traffic

---

## LinkedIn Version A (Short)

AI agents don't replace your team. They give your team superpowers.

We just launched [Product Name], and early customers are seeing 40% faster response times and 3x more cases resolved per agent.

The future of customer service isn't fewer humans. It's better-equipped humans.

What's the biggest bottleneck in your support operation?

#AI #CustomerService #ProductLaunch

---

## LinkedIn Version B (Thought Leadership)

Here's what most companies get wrong about AI in customer service:

They automate the wrong things.

Automating password resets? Good. Automating complex escalations? Dangerous.

The companies seeing real results are the ones that drew a clear line: routine tasks go to AI, relationship tasks stay with humans.

That's exactly what we built [Product Name] to do. And early results from customers like Acme Corp show it works: 82% deflection, 4.8/5 CSAT.

The question isn't "should we use AI?" It's "where should the handoff happen?"

#AI #CustomerExperience #Automation

---

## Twitter/X Version

AI agents that know when to help and when to hand off.

That's [Product Name]. Early customers seeing 40% faster resolutions.

#AI #CX

---

## Analysis

**What works:**
- Version A: Direct, specific metrics, ends with engagement question
- Version B: Contrarian hook ("what most companies get wrong"), longer thought leadership format
- Twitter: Stripped to essentials, one metric, clear value prop

**Platform differences:**
- LinkedIn: More context, storytelling, hashtags (3-5)
- Twitter: Punchy, one stat, minimal hashtags (1-2)
