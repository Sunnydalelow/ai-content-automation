# Customer Story Blog Template (~1,200 words)

**Format:** Proof-oriented customer story as blog post

---

## Metadata
- **Title**: [Product Value Proposition]: How [Customer] Achieved [Metric 1] and [Metric 2]
- **Customer**: [Company name]
- **Industry**: [Industry]
- **Products Used**: [List of products]
- **Builder/Stakeholder**: [Name and role]
- **Date**: [Publication date]
- **Meta Description**: [Customer]'s [role] used [product]'s [key feature] to reach [metric 1] and [metric 2]. Get the strategic details.

---

## Writing Checklist

### Do:
- Use active voice
- Vary sentence length (mix short and long)
- Lead with customer actions and results
- Use simple, direct verbs ("use," "build," "prove")
- Integrate quotes naturally into paragraphs
- Focus on proof and validation
- Include specific numbers and ranges

### Don't:
- Use "not just X, but Y" structures
- Overuse em dashes or colons
- Say "leverage" or "utilize" when "use" works
- Lead with company background/boilerplate
- Isolate quotes in blockquotes
- Use vague language ("many," "significant") without numbers
