## Platform-Specific Best Practices Reminder

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone, thought leadership
- Industry insights and trends
- Employee and company stories
- B2B content performs well
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8am-2pm

### Twitter/X
- Conversational, timely, engaging
- News and real-time updates
- Threads for deeper dives
- Use mentions and replies
- Best times: Weekdays, 9am-3pm

### Facebook
- Community-building, storytelling
- Visual content performs best
- Longer-form stories work
- Video gets high engagement
- Best times: Wed-Fri, 9am-2pm

### Instagram
- Visual-first, authentic, aspirational
- Stories for behind-the-scenes
- Reels for reach
- Carousel for education
- Best times: Wed, 11am & Fri, 10am-11am
