# Customer Story Workflow

## Overview
Customer stories are interview-based case studies (800-1200 words) showcasing customer success. This workflow emphasizes authenticity, customer collaboration, and measurable results.

**Timeline**: 3-6 weeks
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer Agent
**Supporting**: Research Agent, Editor Agent, Customer Success team

---

## Phase 1: Customer Identification (3-7 days)

### Step 1: Customer Selection
**Owner**: PMM with Customer Success

**Selection criteria**:
- [ ] Strong, measurable results
- [ ] Willing to participate and go on record
- [ ] Represents target persona or industry
- [ ] Uses relevant products/features
- [ ] Compelling transformation story
- [ ] Available for interview

**Activities**:
- Review customer success metrics
- Check NPS and satisfaction scores
- Validate with account team
- Confirm no churn risk
- Get initial interest confirmation

**Output**: Selected customer contact

**Time**: 3-5 days

---

### Step 2: Initial Outreach
**Owner**: Customer Success or PMM

- [ ] Send initial request email
- [ ] Explain opportunity and benefits
- [ ] Share example customer stories
- [ ] Propose timeline and requirements
- [ ] Get preliminary commitment
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders to interview

**Output**: Customer agreement to participate

**Time**: 2-7 days (depends on customer response)

---

## Phase 2: Research & Preparation (3-5 days)

### Step 3: Customer Research
**Owner**: Research Agent

Reference: `/agents/researcher.md`

**Research areas**:
- [ ] Company background and industry
- [ ] Business model and size
- [ ] Their customers and market
- [ ] Industry challenges
- [ ] Competitive landscape
- [ ] Public information about their use of your company
- [ ] Any existing testimonials or quotes

**Output**: Customer research brief

**Time**: 2-3 days

---

### Step 4: Internal Alignment
**Owner**: PMM

- [ ] Meet with account team
- [ ] Review customer journey
- [ ] Understand implementation details
- [ ] Gather product usage data
- [ ] Collect preliminary metrics
- [ ] Identify success factors
- [ ] Note any sensitivities

**Output**: Internal briefing document

**Time**: 1-2 days

---

### Step 5: Interview Preparation
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer

- [ ] Draft interview questions
- [ ] Customize based on research
- [ ] Prepare metrics to validate
- [ ] Identify quote opportunities
- [ ] Schedule interview (60-90 minutes)
- [ ] Send pre-interview brief to customer
- [ ] Confirm recording permission

**Output**: Interview guide and scheduled interview

**Time**: 1 day

---

## Phase 3: Interview & Validation (1-2 weeks)

### Step 6: Conduct Interview
**Owner**: PMM or Customer Story Writer

**Interview structure** (60-90 minutes):
- [ ] Introductions and context (5 minutes)
- [ ] Background questions (10 minutes)
- [ ] Challenge discussion (15-20 minutes)
- [ ] Decision process (10-15 minutes)
- [ ] Implementation experience (10-15 minutes)
- [ ] Results and impact (15-20 minutes)
- [ ] Future plans (5-10 minutes)
- [ ] Wrap-up and next steps (5 minutes)

**During interview**:
- Record (with permission)
- Take detailed notes
- Ask for specific metrics
- Get quotable soundbites
- Request permission for specific data points
- Discuss approval process

Reference: `/agents/customer-story-writer.md` for question bank

**Output**: Interview recording and notes

**Time**: 60-90 minutes

---

### Step 7: Fact Gathering
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer with Account Team

- [ ] Collect specific metrics and KPIs
- [ ] Verify timelines
- [ ] Confirm product usage details
- [ ] Validate technical implementation
- [ ] Gather any missing information
- [ ] Request customer logo/photos if available

**Output**: Complete fact set

**Time**: 2-3 days

---

## Phase 4: Writing (1 week)

### Step 8: Story Development
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer

Reference:
- `/templates/customer-story-template.md`
- `/agents/customer-story-writer.md`
- Interview transcript and notes

**Writing process**:
- [ ] Transcribe key quotes
- [ ] Identify 3-4 strongest metrics
- [ ] Outline challenge-solution-results narrative
- [ ] Draft compelling headline
- [ ] Write first draft following template
- [ ] Include 2-4 authentic customer quotes
- [ ] Balance quantitative and qualitative results
- [ ] Add forward-looking vision section
- [ ] Write strong CTA

**Output**: First draft

**Time**: 3-5 days

---

### Step 9: Internal Review
**Owner**: Editor Agent

- [ ] Brand compliance check
- [ ] Fact verification
- [ ] Quote accuracy
- [ ] Story structure and flow
- [ ] Completeness check
- [ ] Grammar and style

**Output**: Internally approved draft

**Time**: 1 day

---

### Step 10: Revisions
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer

- [ ] Address editorial feedback
- [ ] Verify facts with account team
- [ ] Polish narrative
- [ ] Prepare clean draft for customer

**Output**: Customer-ready draft

**Time**: 1 day

---

## Phase 5: Customer Review (1-2 weeks)

### Step 11: Customer Approval Process
**Owner**: PMM or Customer Success

**Submission**:
- [ ] Send draft to customer stakeholders
- [ ] Include cover note explaining review process
- [ ] Set timeline for feedback (1 week)
- [ ] Offer to discuss by phone if needed
- [ ] Request specific approval items:
  - Company name and logo usage
  - All quotes and attributions
  - Metrics and numbers
  - Proprietary information
  - Publication permission

**Output**: Customer feedback

**Time**: 5-10 days (depends on customer)

---

### Step 12: Customer Revisions
**Owner**: Customer Story Writer

- [ ] Incorporate customer edits
- [ ] Verify factual corrections
- [ ] Adjust sensitive information
- [ ] Re-check brand compliance after edits
- [ ] Prepare revised draft

**Output**: Revised draft

**Time**: 1-2 days

---

### Step 13: Final Customer Approval
**Owner**: PMM

- [ ] Send final draft for approval
- [ ] Get written approval (email sufficient)
- [ ] Document approval in project files
- [ ] Confirm publication timeline
- [ ] Thank customer for participation

**Output**: Signed-off customer story with approval

**Time**: 2-5 days

## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Customer unresponsive
**Solution**:
- Engage through Customer Success
- Offer multiple scheduling options
- Keep requests simple and time-bound
- Highlight benefits to them

### Challenge: Lack of hard metrics
**Solution**:
- Use time-based improvements ("reduced from X to Y")
- Qualitative benefits with context
- Before/after comparisons
- Customer satisfaction data

### Challenge: Customer changes mind during review
**Solution**:
- Set expectations upfront
- Build in review time
- Be flexible with edits
- Have backup customer if critical

### Challenge: Long approval cycles
**Solution**:
- Identify decision-maker early
- Provide specific deadline
- Offer discussion call
- Send friendly reminders

### Challenge: Customer wants heavy edits
**Solution**:
- Understand concerns
- Balance authenticity with comfort
- Protect story quality
- Know when to walk away gracefully

## Customer Communication Templates

### Initial Outreach Email
```
Subject: Feature [Company] as a your company Customer Success Story

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Company's] success with [your company Product],
and I'm impressed by [specific achievement].

We'd love to feature [Company] in a customer success story to
share how you've [achieved result]. This would include:
- A 60-minute interview about your journey
- A published case study on your company.com
- Promotion through our channels (reaching [X] audience)
- Assets you can use for your own marketing

Here's an example: [link to similar story]

Would you be interested in exploring this? I'd be happy to
discuss details and answer any questions.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Draft Submission Email
```
Subject: [Company] Customer Story - Draft for Your Review

Hi [Name],

Thank you again for sharing [Company's] story with us. I've
attached the draft customer story for your review.

Please review for:
✓ Accuracy of all facts and metrics
✓ Approval of quotes attributed to you
✓ Comfort with company information shared
✓ Any sensitive or confidential items to adjust

Timeline: Would appreciate feedback by [date] so we can publish
[target date]. Happy to discuss by phone if that's easier.

Let me know if you have any questions or need clarification on
anything.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## Quality Checklist

Before customer submission:
- [ ] Compelling headline with customer + result
- [ ] Clear challenge-solution-results narrative
- [ ] 3+ specific, measurable results
- [ ] 2-4 authentic customer quotes
- [ ] All facts verified internally
- [ ] Products correctly named
- [ ] Brand compliance check passed
- [ ] 800-1,200 words
- [ ] Template fully followed

Before publication:
- [ ] Written customer approval obtained
- [ ] Final legal/compliance check if needed
- [ ] All edits incorporated
- [ ] Final proofread complete
- [ ] SEO optimized
- [ ] Assets prepared
- [ ] Publication scheduled

---

*Customer stories are powerful because they're authentic. Take the time to get the story right and make the customer comfortable. A great customer story builds trust and drives business impact.*
