# Whitepaper Workflow (Long-form)

## Overview
Whitepapers are authoritative long-form guides (3000-5000 words) that establish thought leadership and generate leads. This workflow emphasizes deep research, multiple reviews, and professional design.

**Timeline**: 4-8 weeks
**Owner**: Whitepaper Writer Agent
**Supporting**: Research Agent, Editor Agent, SMEs, Design team, Legal (if needed)

---

## Phase 1: Planning & Strategy (1 week)

### Step 1: Topic Definition
**Owner**: PMM

**Define**:
- [ ] Core topic and angle
- [ ] Target audience (personas)
- [ ] Business objectives (thought leadership, lead gen, etc.)
- [ ] Key differentiators or unique perspective
- [ ] Competitive landscape
- [ ] Success metrics

**Questions to answer**:
- What problem does this solve?
- Why is your company uniquely positioned to write this?
- Who will find this valuable?
- What action should readers take?

**Output**: Topic brief with objectives

**Time**: 1-2 days

---

### Step 2: Stakeholder Alignment
**Owner**: PMM

- [ ] Identify executive sponsor
- [ ] Brief key stakeholders
- [ ] Get buy-in on topic and approach
- [ ] Confirm resources (SMEs, design, etc.)
- [ ] Agree on timeline and milestones
- [ ] Set review process expectations

**Output**: Approved project plan

**Time**: 2-3 days

---

### Step 3: Research Planning
**Owner**: Research Agent with Whitepaper Writer

- [ ] Define research questions
- [ ] Identify required sources
- [ ] Plan SME interviews
- [ ] List data/statistics needed
- [ ] Determine original research needs
- [ ] Create research timeline

**Output**: Research plan

**Time**: 1-2 days

## Common Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Research takes too long
**Solution**:
- Scope research upfront
- Set research deadlines
- Prioritize must-have vs nice-to-have
- Use research agent efficiently

### Challenge: Too many stakeholder opinions
**Solution**:
- Designate single decision-maker
- Set clear review criteria
- Consolidate feedback
- Stick to timeline

### Challenge: Design delays
**Solution**:
- Brief designers early
- Provide clear specifications
- Build in buffer time
- Have approval process ready

### Challenge: Scope creep
**Solution**:
- Lock outline before writing
- Set page/word limits
- Stay focused on core topic
- Save extras for future content
